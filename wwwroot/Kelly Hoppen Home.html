Description: In Kelly Hoppen Home, Kelly Hoppen shares her professional expertise from the new perspective of having been her own client.Here, she reveals how to create a home with first-hand experience of the problems encountered and challenges faced on both sides of the decorating fence. This book covers every aspect of designing and decorating a home, from reconfiguring space to making decorating and furnishing choices, to creating mood and adding flourishes of individual style that express your personality and make your home uniquely your own.

Beginning with her personal story of redesigning her own home, Kelly explains how to be your own interior designer.
