Description: Atlas of Office Interiors is a compendium of the best interior designs for offices over the last six years. This book presents a carefully chosen and extensive selection of offices, which reflect the most-up-to-date trends and ideas in designs for work spaces. The office, the quint essential working environment where employees invest most of their working life, is generating growing interest among architects and interior designers. The global economy, competing markets, and the evolution of new technologies have inspired new ways of working, which have given rise to a revision of the traditional concept of office design.

This book is organized in four chapters: large offices, medium-size offices, small offices and offices in the home. 600 illustrated pages, including graphical documentation, offer interior design lovers the opportunity to enjoy these ingenious designs.
