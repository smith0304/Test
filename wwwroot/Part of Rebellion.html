Description: Flying Fortress :
Munich-based artist Flying Fortress borrows his catchy name from the heavily armed World War II bomber planes that have fascinated him ever since putting together model aircrafts as a child. Today he assembles his signature cartoon characters as well as ads and logos for big business. His background as a graffiti artist allows him to fulfill corporate demands while never giving up his own artistic independence. This results in artwork for famous clients like Stussy or Fenchurch and the participation in art projects around the world.

The Teddy Troops are Flying Fortress most famous creation. They have invaded cities around the world, attracting attention not only by their sheer abundance but mainly by their friendly yet ambiguous looks. Being featured on stickers, walls, canvas, t-shirts and vinyl figures, the Teddy Troops have became a brand of their own that easily fulfills their creator¡¯s mission: make people think and smile! But Fortress visual creativity reaches a lot further and has earned him an international reputation that lead to exhibitions and artistic collaborations around the world.
