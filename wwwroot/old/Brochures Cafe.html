Description:328 pages hardcover.Welcome to the second edition of "From North to South America". This time, it is all about brochures. This book reviews the best contemporary brochures made by designers in north, south and central America. It is an inspirational and essential reference tool for anyone working in media, communications and design firms. You will find folders, catalogues, brochures and all kinds of printed materials all of them very original and conceptual. Most designers thought that the Internet would spell the end of printed material. However, today we find brochures that are even more original and full of soul and contemporary style, proving the difference between real and digital materials.
