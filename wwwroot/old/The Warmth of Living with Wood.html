Description: Wood has very many positive attributes as a natural construction material, but was for a long time neglected in the architecture of Western Europe. The most important reason for selecting wood, though, is the pleasant atmosphere enjoyed by owners of wooden houses. This also applies when wood is used as an essential element of an interior: floors, paneling and fitted cabinetwork creates a welcoming feeling of warmth and coziness in a home.

This book features many inspiring examples of both houses and interiors: homes constructed out of wood and also traditional brick buildings where wood occupies a prominent position inside the house.
