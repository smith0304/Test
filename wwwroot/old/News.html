I first met Jianping He in Toyama, Japan in 2000 during the opening of the poster triennial exhibition where I was a member of the international jury. Jianping struck me as very interesting without having a brash personality, and since then our paths have crossed on numerous occasions. Jianping He has found a route into European design culture in a way few other Asian designers have. He is equally at home playing around with the idioms and characteristics of European design as he is with those of Chinese design, which forges a design dialogue between the two, so to speak. This is not merely due to Jianping He having studied both in Germany and China, but rather in his open-minded interest in the manifold facets of design, which has seen him travel across the globe, and his constantly seeking dialogue and debate with his contemporaries.
