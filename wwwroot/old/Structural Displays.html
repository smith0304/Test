Description: This book is a collection of simple, novel and out-of-the ordinary packaging and displays. Some are as simple as a single die-cut, while others have up to nine different structures in one packaging. Best of all, the different possibilities are characterized by their creativity in finding packaging and design solutions. Making it an easy and organized reference, the displays are arranged and classified into three levels of technical difficulty. Like its predecessors Structural Packaging and
Structural Greetings, Structural Displays is a must-have book for design professionals.
