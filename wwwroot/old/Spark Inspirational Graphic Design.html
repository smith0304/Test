Description: Graphic design has become one of the most influential professions around the world. As visual influences impact on our lives more now than ever before, technology makes this creative community appear smaller and smaller and our source of inspiration for visual production shifts from local communities to an ever-expanding global network.

What makes good design? We can now judge that in real time, as websites, blogs and online communities become the meeting place and ideas travel across the planet in seconds.

Spark. Inspirational Graphic Design is a collection of work by creative individuals from this media-savvy global age who are doing things on their own terms. They inspired , and they inspire.
