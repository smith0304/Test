Description: Fashion Design Drawing by Zeshu Takamura is targetted at beginners in illustration and is based on the curriculum of his beginner's course at the fashion design school that he teaches in. This book is a must-have for all fashion design students who require a basic knowledge of drawing and illustration techniques.
Zeshu Takamura graduated from Dress Design Department at Kuwasawa Design Laboratory and studied under Reiko Saito, a fashion illustrator. After graduation, he became a freelance illustrator and is now an assistant professor of Bunka Women's University.
