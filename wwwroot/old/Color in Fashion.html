Description: Color is king on the catwalks and in the streets, and features as much in bold combinations as it does in one overall color. These pages, brimming with beautiful photographs, are categorized by individual garments and provide an insight into the way different contemporary designers use color. Thus, as well as showing various interpretations of the same basic model, they demonstrate how color can alter the perception of a design and how its creative use can transform the way the world of work and its surroundings look.
