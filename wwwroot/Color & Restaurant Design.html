Description: This book aims to find exclusive architectural concepts that contributes to discussions of contemporary culinary art. These are designs that turn the restaurant into much more than a delight for the taste buds; it allows the sense of sight to participate in the unique experience that eating out can become under the right conditions. There is a common denominator to all the projects in this book: color is used to create atmosphere and give a sense of identity to the restaurants featured. This is a catalogue of establishments with spectacular deign that are based on color, particularly seen in furnishings and lighting systems.
