From the multifunction building to the regular apartment building, the project presented in this book, have one common point, the research of concepts and innovative distribution of the space looking for optimizing at its most the possibilities of available space and the program.

The work are presented in a clear and organized manner and the projects¡¯ are classified by architecture types. Also includes a theory part where the main guidelines are reviewed (measurements, distribution concepts, considerations that have to be taken in account and so on) and how they are applied in the projects featured. All plans and descriptive texts have been realized together with the architects themselves enabling in this way the best understanding of each project displayed for each of the projects displayed. Making this a unique reference book for any professional on the architectural and construction.
